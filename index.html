Summary

Despite spending the better part of a decade in teaching, I've decided that this is not the career path for me.

I am truly passionate about working with students in helping them to learn, grow and find passion within the subject I teach - English Literature, but the profession has been overlooked and underfunded for so long, that I feel a career with real opportunity and progression lies eslewhere.

As you may of guessed, that 'elsewhere' is in web development!
